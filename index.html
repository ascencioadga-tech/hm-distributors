Our Story

A Family Legacy of Fresh Produce

HM Distributors was founded in 1985 in Nogales, Arizona, by Humberto Jose Monteverde and his sons — a team who saw an opportunity to bring the finest produce from Mexico to the U.S. market.

From humble beginnings, Humberto worked tirelessly to build a thriving business that prioritizes quality and customer service above all. His dedication to excellence established the foundation for what would become one of the region's most trusted distributors.

Today, HM Distributors is one of the leading produce distributors in the southwestern United States, and remains a proud family-owned and operated business.
